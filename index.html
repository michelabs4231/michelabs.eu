Who we are

A new chapter for
mobility in Europe

Michelangelo Labs SRL is a Romanian subsidiary purpose-built to spearhead the expansion of cutting-edge car-sharing services across Romania and the wider European market. Headquartered in the Western Region, we are part of a broader ecosystem of innovation that connects vehicle owners and drivers through technology.

We believe that access to transportation is a fundamental driver of social and economic growth. Our mission is to make shared mobility accessible, affordable, and impactful — for individuals, communities, and businesses alike.

Founded to serve as the European gateway, Michelangelo Labs combines local market expertise with proven technology to build a network of shared vehicles that works for everyone.

What we do

Expanding shared mobility,
one city at a time

We deploy, grow and localize vehicle-sharing solutions across Romania and Europe, working with individuals, businesses and municipalities.

🗺️

Market Expansion

We identify high-potential urban and peri-urban areas in Romania and across Europe, building local density of vehicles and users to trigger organic growth.

🤝

B2B Partnerships

We partner with car rental companies, corporate fleets, and dealers to integrate them into the sharing economy, unlocking new revenue streams through our white-label solution.

⚙️

Technology Deployment

We localise and deploy our AI-powered platform — adapting language, regulation, and user experience to each market for seamless adoption.

🔒

Insurance Innovation

Our proprietary insurance solution, covering cars, motorcycles, recreational vehicles and vans, travels with the platform — removing one of the biggest barriers to car-sharing adoption.

🏙️

Municipal Collaboration

We work with city authorities and regional bodies to integrate shared mobility into urban planning, reducing congestion and parking pressure in growing cities.

📊

Data & Impact Reporting

Every ride generates insights. We track environmental, social, and economic impact using a proprietary SROI model developed in partnership with Bocconi University.

Recognition

Selected for inVest —
Romania's first Urban Mobility Accelerator

In 2025, Volvero was selected among 14 teams from over 50 European applicants to participate in the inaugural edition of inVest, Romania's first accelerator dedicated to urban mobility innovation.

EIT Urban Mobility ADR Vest · 2025

Startup Track — Western Region of Romania

inVest is implemented by Iceberg+ and Cowork Timișoara, with financial support from EIT Urban Mobility (European Institute of Innovation and Technology) for Stage 1 and ADR Vest (West Regional Development Agency) for Stage 2.

Our participation confirmed what we already knew: the Western Region of Romania — covering Timiș, Arad, Caraș-Severin and Hunedoara — is a strategic entry point for shared mobility expansion in Central-Eastern Europe. The accelerator fast-tracked our local network, regulatory insight, and access to key institutional partners.
